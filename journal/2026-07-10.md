## Notes / Reflections

- 2026-07-10: Received user feedback from initial Falcon A1 Pro laser test.
  - Alignment Issue: PNG/SVG sidecars currently lack a shared, explicit bounding box, causing printer software to auto-scale/auto-center incorrectly. Need to enforce a rigid canvas (e.g., 300x300mm bed size) for both formats.
  - Kerf & Margins: Cuts are nearly 3mm wide and overlapping text. Need to implement a `minimum_cut_clearance` assert in the pipeline to prevent cut paths from encroaching on text bounding boxes.
  - Resolution & Ghosting: The 30mm coins are too small for the laser spot size, causing fine details to blur. Successive passes ghost. We need to scale the coins up (e.g., 40-50mm), increase font sizes, reduce the number of coins per page, and rely on single-pass operations.
