# Material Safety

- Never process unknown-composition material.
- Do not process PVC, vinyl, chlorine-containing plastics, explosive materials, or mirrored/highly reflective surfaces.
- Treat every unverified recipe as calibration-only.
- Provide ventilation, active supervision, air assist where specified, and suitable fire response equipment.
- Run non-emitting framing before every job.
- Treat operation-stage G-code files as full operation runs: rerunning `operations/*run_N_passes.gcode` adds all `N` passes again.
- Stop immediately on flame, unexpected smoke, material movement, collision risk, or machine alarm.
